Cyber Safety Sections

Awareness

Awareness helps you identify phishing, scams, malware, and online fraud. Stay informed about common threats to stay protected.

Prevention

Use strong passwords, enable 2FA, update software, and avoid suspicious links. Simple steps can prevent cyber attacks.

Report

Report cybercrimes at cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930. Always keep screenshots and evidence before reporting.
