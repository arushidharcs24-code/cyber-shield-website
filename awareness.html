Stay Safe in the Digital World

CyberShield educates all age groups about cyber threats, safe browsing habits, and digital responsibility.

Cyber Awareness

Phishing Attacks

  • Fake emails & messages
  • Steal login credentials
  • Impersonate trusted brands

Malware & Viruses

  • Damages devices
  • Steals private data
  • Spreads via unsafe downloads

Strong Passwords

  • Use long & unique passwords
  • Avoid personal info
  • Enable 2FA

Prevention

Children & Teenagers

  • Avoid talking to strangers online
  • Report cyberbullying
  • Do not share personal info

Students

  • Beware of fake job offers
  • Protect academic accounts
  • Avoid online scams

Working Professionals

  • Identify phishing emails
  • Protect company data
  • Use secure networks

Senior Citizens

  • Avoid banking frauds
  • Never share OTP or PIN
  • Verify before trusting calls

Report Fraud

If you encounter a cybercrime or fraud attempt, report it immediately to local authorities or online platforms. Keep screenshots and logs as proof.
